The Empowerverse Plan

The key to success is reaching product market fit.

The Empowerverse plan is a holistic strategy for achieving success through product-market fit. Anchored by Holy Vible WhitePill, it fosters a positive digital environment with features like InstaRama and Real Device, redirecting users from mindless scrolling. Health and fulfillment take center stage with platforms such as Healthy Social Inspirationals, EmpowerFeed, and Fulfillfeed, while initiatives like Get Off Our App and Bloom Scrolling Social Patch encourage a healthier digital relationship. From Energy Feed to Divine Social, the plan integrates various channels, providing uplifting content, fostering connections, and promoting gratitude. Exclusive experiences like the Prime Program and Prime Holy Grail round out the approach, creating an empowered and enriching digital space for users.

Our co-founder previously helped make products go viral by testing lots of different types of content and sticking with what clicked. We know how powerful organic virality can be, and the meritocracy of tiktok is great for testing memetic fitness of branding / ads.

After proving the need of this video feed and attracting super-users at a small scale, we are raising capital to lead this market.

In order to split-test properly, we are cloning our backend across multiple different brand styles to split-test bringing this to market. This allows us to spread our net wide, finding the right brand for the market.

On top of that, we are building a master Empowerverse app, inside of the Empowerverse app every sub-category can be found and utilized just like Reddit.

This allows users to engage with more than just one type of empowering video. Whether needing inspiration to work out, to increase gratitude in life, prefer futuristic vibes, or a community made up of your religion. Empowerverse covers it all.

This allows us to better serve our customers needs, prior to reaching critical mass of users when we have more data to correlate and predict a user’s needs / interest.

We believe this is also a healthier way for users to engage with content. As a single generalized algorithm takes away any user choice. If you do not choose what you consume, you do not choose who you become.
